Navigating the **my wood farm latest update** requires a structured approach to spending. Many new players make the mistake of expanding their plot too early, leaving them with a large, empty farm and no tools to fill it. Follow this optimized path to ensure steady growth.

  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Cash Loop">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize Axe Output">
    Invest your first significant earnings into a Rare axe upgrade. A faster axe means more wood per swing, which compounds your income for every subsequent sale.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maximize Production Density">
    Before buying more land, fill your current plot with as many axes as possible. Having multiple axes working on different trees simultaneously is the most cost-effective way to boost production.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Strategic Plot Expansion">
    Once your current plot is "crowded" and you have no room for new axes, purchase a plot expansion. This opens up space for tougher targets like Stumps and eventually Giant Trees.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Endgame Optimization">
    Transition your entire farm to Legendary and Magical axes. At this stage, you should be focusing on the highest-value targets and maximizing your offline production rates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Common Pitfall">
