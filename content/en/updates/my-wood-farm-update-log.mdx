<Section icon={Shield} title="Offline Wood Farming & Passive Earnings">

One of the most powerful mechanics tracked in the **my wood farm update log** is the offline farming system. Your axes do not stop working when you close Roblox; they continue to harvest timber passively. 

When you return to the game, you can immediately claim this stored wood, sell it for a massive cash injection, and fund your next round of upgrades. However, your offline yield is directly capped by the quality and placement of the tools left on your plot.

| Optimization Step | Action Required | Direct Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Pre-Logout Check | Spend all loose cash on axe upgrades and place them on active spawns | Maximizes the passive harvesting rate per minute while away |
| Inventory Clear | Ensure your wood storage is empty before logging off | Prevents potential capped-yield bugs during long offline periods |
| Return Routine | Immediately head to the sell zone upon logging back in | Converts passive wood stockpile into usable cash instantly |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Passive Strategy">
To calculate your exact passive efficiency, record your wood total right before logging out. When you return, subtract that starting number from your new total and divide it by the hours you were away to find your hourly yield.
</Callout>

By systematically upgrading your tools before taking breaks, you turn downtime into progress. This passive loop is highly effective for players who cannot commit to long, active grinding sessions but still want to stay competitive as the game updates.
