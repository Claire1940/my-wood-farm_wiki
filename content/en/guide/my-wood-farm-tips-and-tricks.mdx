<Section icon={Trophy} title="Offline Earnings and Rebirth Strategy">

One of the best features of My Wood Farm is the ability to earn while you sleep. Your axes continue to harvest wood even when you are not logged into Roblox. However, the rate of offline production is based on your current equipment levels at the moment you log off.

Rebirth is the final mechanic to master. When you rebirth, you lose your current axes and cash in exchange for permanent multipliers. Knowing when to reset is the difference between a top-tier player and a casual one.

**Offline Earnings Formula:**
- **Passive Wood** = (Total Axe Damage per Second) × (Minutes Offline) × (0.5 Multiplier)
- **Note**: Most simulators apply a 50% penalty to offline rates to encourage active play.

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
