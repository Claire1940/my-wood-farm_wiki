To progress efficiently, you must carefully calculate where to spend your cash. Buying land too early leaves you with empty space that yields no income, while hoarding cash without upgrading your axe slows down your active chopping speed.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Cash Loop">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Upgrade Active Axe Power">
    Spend your first savings on direct axe upgrades. Improving your active tool ensures you clear regular trees faster, dramatically boosting cash-per-minute rates.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Increase Production Density">
    Purchase and place additional axes on your current plot. Filling these slots allows multiple axes to work simultaneously, multiplying both online and offline gains.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Expand the Plot">
    Once your current plot is fully populated with working axes and you lack space for more, buy your first plot expansion to open up more production positions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Target High-Durability Nodes">

<Section icon={Swords} title="Best Money Farming Methods">

Maximizing your cash flow requires balancing active gameplay with passive offline systems. By utilizing both, you can maintain steady progression whether you are actively playing or taking a break.

### Active Chopping vs. Passive Farming
Active chopping is highly lucrative during short, focused sessions. Move constantly between available trees on your plot to minimize downtime. For passive progression, focus on optimizing axe placement.

| Farming Method | Active Effort | Ideal Session Length | Primary Focus |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Active Chopping** | High | 10–30 minutes | Target regular trees, quick sell cycles |
| **Plot Automation** | Low | 1–2 hours (Semi-AFK) | Maximize axe density, clear paths |
| **Offline Farming** | None | Overnight / Work breaks | Equip highest tier axes before logout |

### Plot Layout Optimization
Keep your axes clustered near the spawning zones of your highest-value targets. Clear the center of your plot of obstacles to maintain an unobstructed pathway between your wood piles and the selling station.

<Callout type="info" title="Offline Earnings Formula">
Your offline earnings are calculated using a simple formula: 
`Offline Wood Gained = Wood Count Upon Return - Wood Count Prior to Logout`. 
Multiply this by your current selling rate to estimate your passive cash generation.
</Callout>
