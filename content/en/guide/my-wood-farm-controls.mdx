<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Beginner Progression">

Once you have mastered the basic **my wood farm controls**, you should follow an optimized progression route. New players often make the mistake of saving cash for large plot expansions too early, which slows down their overall income. Follow this step-by-step pipeline to establish a highly profitable loop.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Start the Cash Loop">
    Equip your starting Wooden Axe. Walk up to the regular trees on your plot and begin clicking or tapping to chop. Collect the dropped wood and walk to the designated sell area to convert it into cash.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Purchase First Axe Upgrade">
    Do not save up for land yet. Take your initial cash directly to the shop and purchase an affordable axe upgrade. Upgraded axes significantly increase your damage per swing, making each tree cycle much faster.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Increase Production Density">
    Purchase and place additional axes on your plot. These automated axes will help chop down trees nearby, dramatically increasing your active output and preparing your farm for passive generation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Expand the Plot">
    Once your current plot is fully occupied with productive axes and you have no space left for upgrades, spend your accumulated cash to purchase adjacent plot land.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Empty Land">
Buying plot expansions too early leaves your cash tied up in empty space. Only expand when your current land is completely filled with working axes.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How Offline Earnings Work">

One of the best mechanics in My Wood Farm is the ability to earn resources while you are away. Your placed axes continue to farm wood automatically when you log out of Roblox, allowing you to return to a massive payday.

To optimize this system, follow this simple before-logout routine:

```
[Upgrade Axes] ➔ [Place Axes Near Trees] ➔ [Record Current Wood] ➔ [Log Out]
```

Upon returning to the game, follow these steps to maximize your growth:

| Step | Action | Purpose |
| :--- | :---: | :--- |
| **1** | Collect offline wood | Claim accumulated passive resources |
| **2** | Sell wood at shop | Convert raw materials into cash |
| **3** | Reinvest earnings | Purchase stronger axes and expand plot |

<Callout type="tip" title="Calculate Your Rates">

`Offline Wood Gained / Minutes Offline = Average Passive Production Rate`.
