<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Best Upgrade Order for Maximum Yield">

To build an efficient empire, you must balance axe power, helper axes, and land space. Spending all your cash on plot expansion early leaves you with empty land and weak tools. Follow this structured upgrade sequence to get the best return on investment.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Optimize Your Main Axe">
    Spend your first few thousand cash on upgrading your personal axe. This ensures your active clicking sessions yield high amounts of wood.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Hire Helper Axes">
    Buy additional automated axes for your plot. These helpers chop automatically, keeping your wood production steady even when you aren't clicking.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Fill Current Plot Space">
    Ensure every available slot on your starter plot has an axe assigned to it before you think about buying more land.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Expand Plot and Scale">
    Purchase your first plot expansion. Immediately fill the new space with mid-tier axes to avoid leaving the ground empty.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Progression Milestone">
Once you have filled your second plot expansion with Rare-tier axes, your passive generation will be strong enough to fund Legendary upgrades with minimal active play.
</Callout>

</Section>

Use this checklist to prepare your farm before logging off:
