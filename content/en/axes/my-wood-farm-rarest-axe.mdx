Reaching the level of the **my wood farm rarest axe** requires a structured spending strategy. Spreading your cash too thin on early plot expansions will leave you with a massive, empty farm and low production power. Follow this optimized upgrade path to maximize your efficiency.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Cash Loop">
    Equip your starting Wooden Axe and chop regular trees on your home plot. Collect the wood and sell it immediately at the sell point to build your starting capital.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Upgrade Active Axe Power">
    Reinvest your first earnings directly into a better active axe. Upgrading your primary tool ensures you clear trees faster, directly increasing your cash-per-minute ratio.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Increase Production Density">
    Purchase and place additional axes on your current plot. Having multiple axes working simultaneously multiplies your active output and drastically boosts your offline production rates.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Expand the Plot">
    Only purchase more land when your current plot is fully occupied with productive axes. Expansion opens up more placement slots for your growing fleet of tools.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Target Endgame Materials">
    With a fully expanded plot and high-tier Legendary or Magical axes, shift your focus entirely to harvesting Tree Stumps and Giant Trees for massive cash returns.
  </Step>
</Steps>
