<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Axe Upgrade Path">

To maximize your return on investment, follow this optimized upgrade sequence. This path ensures you never run out of cash while transitioning between different tree types.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Cash Loop">
    Equip your starting Wooden Axe and chop regular trees on your home plot. Collect the wood and sell it immediately at the sell point to build your initial cash reserve.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire First Rare Upgrade">
    Purchase your first Rare-tier axe upgrade as soon as you can afford it. This instantly lowers the hits required to fell regular trees, doubling your active income.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Optimize Plot Density">
    Instead of buying more land, purchase additional axes of your highest unlocked tier and place them on your current plot to automate wood collection.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Expand the Plot">
    Once your current plot is fully populated with high-stat axes, spend your cash to expand your plot. This opens up more tree spawning points and space for more axes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Transition to Magical Gear">
    Use your massive passive income to purchase Magical Axes. Position these high-stat tools near durable targets like Tree Stumps and Giant Trees for maximum output.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Check">
A good rule of thumb is to expand your plot only when you have enough spare cash to immediately place at least one new high-tier axe on the newly opened land.
</Callout>
