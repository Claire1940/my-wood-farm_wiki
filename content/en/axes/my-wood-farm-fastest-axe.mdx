Owning the **my wood farm fastest axe** is only half the battle. To truly optimize your farm, you must consider the placement and the specific upgrades available in the shop. Efficiency in My Wood Farm is measured by "Wood Per Second" (WPS). By positioning your fastest axes near the highest-yield targets, you minimize the "travel time" for the AI axes and maximize active uptime.

| Upgrade Type | Effect on Speed | Priority | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Swing Speed** | Increases hits per second | Very High | General wood farming |
| **Plot Expansion** | Allows more axes | Medium | Late-game scaling |

However, once you reach the Magical tier and start facing Giant Trees with millions of health points, damage per hit becomes a vital stat to prevent "stalling" on a single target.
