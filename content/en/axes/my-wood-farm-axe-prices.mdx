<Section icon={Target} title="Optimal Upgrade Order and Spending Strategy">

To make the most of your cash, you need a clear plan for managing **my wood farm axe prices**. Buying every upgrade the moment it becomes available isn't always the most efficient path. Sometimes, it is better to save for a higher-tier axe that offers a bigger jump in production rather than spending on incremental improvements.

Follow this step-by-step progression to ensure you are spending your hard-earned cash wisely:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Cash Loop">
    Use your starting wooden axe to clear basic trees. Do not spend cash on anything until you can afford your first significant axe upgrade. This ensures your base production rate is established.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize Axe Power">
    Upgrade your primary axe to the Rare tier. A stronger axe clears targets faster, meaning you spend less time clicking and more time collecting wood. This is the most effective way to boost your hourly earnings.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Fill the Plot">
    Before expanding your land, ensure every available slot on your current plot is occupied by the best axes you can afford. Multiple axes working simultaneously will drastically increase your wood yield.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Strategic Expansion">
    Only purchase plot expansion when you have more axes than space. Expansion is a "passive" upgrade that only pays off when you fill the new land with productive tools.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Push for Magical Tier">
    Once your plot is full and your income is steady, begin saving for Legendary and eventually Magical axes. These are the final steps in the current progression and will maximize your offline earnings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Spending Trap">
Avoid buying too much land too early. An empty plot generates zero revenue. Always ensure you have enough cash to buy axes for the new land you just unlocked.
</Callout>
