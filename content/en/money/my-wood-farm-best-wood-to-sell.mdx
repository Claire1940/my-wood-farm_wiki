<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Processing for Maximum Value">

Selling raw logs is a common mistake made by beginners. To get the absolute most out of your harvest, you must process the logs. The sawmill is the primary processing tool in My Wood Farm. By running raw logs through a sawmill, you convert them into planks, which increases their market value. Upgraded sawmills offer even higher multipliers and process wood at a much faster rate.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Harvest the Logs">
    Locate the highest-tier tree your axe can cut. Chop it down and cut the trunk into manageable segments.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Load and Transport">
    Load the log segments onto your vehicle. Secure them properly to prevent them from falling off during transit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Sawmill Processing">
    Feed the logs into your sawmill. Wait for the machine to refine the raw wood into polished planks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Sell Step">
    Collect the processed planks from the output tray and deposit them into the sell zone to claim your gold.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Processing efficiency is determined by your sawmill tier. While the basic sawmill is free, it has a slow processing speed and a lower value multiplier. Investing in an Industrial Sawmill or a Plasma Processor is highly recommended for active players looking to optimize their workflow.

| Processor Type | Cost to Unlock | Value Multiplier | Processing Speed |
| Basic Sawmill | Free | 1.5x | Slow |
| Advanced Sawmill | 5,000 Gold | 2.0x | Medium |
| Industrial Sawmill | 25,000 Gold | 2.5x | Fast |
| Plasma Processor | 100,000 Gold | 3.0x | Instant |
