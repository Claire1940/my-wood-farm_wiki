<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Calculate Offline Earnings">

One of the best mechanics in the game is offline farming. Your axes continue to harvest wood while you are away, allowing you to return to a massive stockpile of resources. Use these simple formulas to track and optimize your passive income.

### Passive Production Formulas

- **Offline Wood Gained** = Wood total after returning - Wood total before leaving
- **Passive Production Rate** = Offline Wood Gained / Total Offline Minutes
- **Estimated Cash Value** = Offline Wood Gained × Current Selling Value per Wood

### Before-Logout Routine
To ensure you maximize your passive returns, complete this checklist before closing the game:

1. Spend all available cash on axe damage and speed upgrades.
2. Ensure every single axe slot on your plot is occupied.
3. Record your starting wood total to track your passive growth rate when you return.

<Callout type="success" title="Offline Bonus">
Because offline yields are calculated based on your active axes, upgrading your gear right before logging off guarantees a much higher passive payout when you return.
