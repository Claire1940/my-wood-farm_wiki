<Section icon={Shield} title="Maximizing Offline Earnings">

One of the most powerful features of this simulator is the ability to earn while you are away. Your axes do not stop working just because you closed the game. However, the rate of offline production is determined by the state of your farm at the moment you disconnect.

To calculate your potential, use this simple formula:
**Offline Wood Gained = (Total Axe Power) x (Time Away)**

| Factor | Impact on Offline | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Axe Tier** | Massive | Upgrade before logging off |
| **Axe Count** | High | Fill all slots before leaving |
| **Tree Type** | Medium | Position near high-durability targets |
| **Plot Size** | Low | Only matters for slot count |

<Callout type="warning" title="Logout Checklist">
Always sell your current wood stash before logging out. If your storage is full, your axes cannot collect more wood while you are offline, effectively wasting your idle time.
</Callout>
