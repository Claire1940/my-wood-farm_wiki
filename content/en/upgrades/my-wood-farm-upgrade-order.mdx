<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Upgrade Path & Spending Rules">

Maximizing your cash flow requires careful budgeting. Follow this step-by-step progression route to ensure you do not waste resources on low-yield upgrades.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Starter Cash Loop">
    Equip your basic Wooden Axe and begin chopping regular trees on your starting plot. Do not buy anything until you have completed several full inventory sales. This initial cash pool is the foundation for your first major tool upgrade.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Upgrade Your Primary Axe">
    Reinvest your first wave of cash directly into an upgraded axe. A stronger tool reduces the number of swings needed per tree, dramatically increasing your active wood output and cash-per-minute ratio.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maximize Plot Density">
    Before purchasing more land, buy and place additional working axes on your current plot. Filling every available slot ensures that multiple tools are harvesting wood simultaneously, boosting both active and offline production.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Expand the Plot Layout">
    Once your starting plot is fully occupied by active axes and you have no room left to place new equipment, spend your cash to unlock adjacent land. This opens up more farming space and introduces tougher, higher-value targets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Acquire Magical Equipment">
    Transition your focus toward high-tier rare, legendary, and magical axes. These specialized tools are designed to tear through high-durability targets like Tree Stumps and Giant Trees, which yield massive cash rewards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Empty Land">
Buying plot expansions when you do not have the spare axes to fill them is a waste of capital. Keep your money in equipment upgrades until space is your absolute limiting factor.
</Callout>

</Section>
