To reach the top of the leaderboards, you must follow a structured path for your **my wood farm production upgrades**. Many players make the mistake of expanding their land too early, leaving them with large, empty plots and no cash to fill them with axes. The most efficient players follow a "Density-First" philosophy, ensuring every square inch of their current land is generating the maximum possible revenue before moving to the next expansion.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Cash Loop">
    Use your starting Wooden Axe to clear the initial trees on your plot. Do not worry about layout yet; just focus on getting enough cash for your first three axe upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Maximize Axe Output">
    Prioritize upgrading the strength and speed of your primary axes. A single strong axe is better than three weak ones because it clears the tree "respawn" timer more effectively.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Increase Production Density">
    Once your primary axes are upgraded, fill every available slot on your current plot. This ensures that even when you aren't actively clicking, your farm is producing wood at 100% capacity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Strategic Plot Expansion">
    Only buy more land when you literally cannot fit another axe on your current plot. Expansion is a one-time cost that unlocks potential, but it provides zero immediate income until axes are placed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Transition to High-Tier Targets">
    With a full plot of upgraded axes, shift your focus to Stumps and Giant Trees. These require more "durability damage" but offer significantly higher cash returns per harvest.
  </Step>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Tree Types and Harvesting Values">

Understanding your targets is just as important as knowing your tools. My Wood Farm features several different types of harvestable timber, each with its own durability rating and wood yield. As you progress through your **my wood farm production upgrades**, you will unlock the ability to tackle larger targets that provide a massive boost to your selling potential.

| Target Type | Durability | Wood Yield | Best Axe for Job |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Regular Tree** | Low | Standard | Any Axe |
| **Tree Stump** | High | 3x Standard | Rare / Legendary |
| **Giant Tree** | Very High | 10x+ Standard | Magical Axes |

Regular trees are your bread and butter during the first few hours of gameplay. They have low health and respawn quickly, making them perfect for rapid-fire selling. However, once you have access to Legendary or Magical axes, you should dedicate a section of your farm specifically to Tree Stumps and Giant Trees. These targets act as "mini-bosses" for your axes, requiring sustained production power but rewarding you with enough wood to skip several minor upgrades at once.

<Callout type="info" title="Targeting Logic">
Your axes will automatically target the nearest available tree. To maximize efficiency, place your strongest axes in the center of the plot where they can reach the high-value Stumps and Giant Trees, while keeping your faster, lower-tier axes on the perimeter for regular tree clearing.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Maximizing Offline Earnings and Passive Gains">

One of the most powerful features of My Wood Farm is the ability to earn while you are not actively playing. Your axes will continue to chop and collect wood even when you are logged off. This makes your **my wood farm production upgrades** even more valuable, as they work for you 24/7. To get the most out of your time away, you should establish a "Logout Routine" that prepares your farm for maximum passive production.

**How to Calculate Your Passive Success:**
- **Step 1:** Record your wood total right before logging off.
- **Step 2:** Note the time you leave.
- **Step 3:** Upon returning, subtract the old total from the new total.
- **Step 4:** Divide the gain by the number of minutes you were away to find your "Passive PPM" (Production Per Minute).

| Session Type | Strategy | Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Active Play** | Manual Chopping | Rapid selling and micro-upgrades |
| **Short Break** | High-Speed Axes | Clearing regular trees for quick cash |
| **Overnight** | High-Yield Axes | Slow clearing of Giant Trees for massive returns |

Before you close the game, spend all your available cash on the highest-yield axe upgrades you can afford. Since speed matters less during long offline sessions than raw yield per tree, prioritize the "Magical" and "Legendary" tiers. Ensure every axe slot is filled, as an empty slot is zero production while you sleep.

<Callout type="tip" title="The Morning Harvest">
When you log back in, your first action should be to sell the massive stockpile of wood your axes have collected. Use this huge influx of cash to buy a major upgrade (like a new plot or a top-tier axe) immediately, as this will set the pace for your next active session.
</Callout>
