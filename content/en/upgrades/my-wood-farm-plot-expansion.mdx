<Section icon={Map} title="My Wood Farm Plot Expansion Basics">

Plot expansion is a fundamental mechanic in **my wood farm** that directly impacts your wood production rate and overall cash flow. While upgrading your axes increases individual chopping power, expanding your plot allows you to place more axes simultaneously. This dual progression system is key to transitioning from active early-game chopping to a highly profitable passive farming setup.

| Expansion Phase | Primary Focus | Main Target | Strategy |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |
| **Early Game** | Starter Wooden Axe | Regular Trees | Quick active sales to fund first upgrades |
| **Mid Game** | Rare & Legendary Axes | Tree Stumps | Density optimization before land purchase |
| **Late Game** | Magical Axes | Giant Trees | Full plot expansion with maximum axe placement |

<Callout type="tip" title="Density Over Size">
Do not rush to buy new land if your current plot has empty space. It is always more cost-effective to fill your existing plot with upgraded axes before paying for land expansion.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Plot Expansion Route">

To build an efficient farm without wasting valuable cash, you should follow a structured development route. This ensures you always have enough liquidity to purchase stronger axes as soon as they are unlocked.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Cash Loop">
    Equip your starting Wooden Axe and chop the regular trees on your plot. Collect the wood and sell it immediately at the sell point to establish your baseline income.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Upgrade Active Axe Power">
    Spend your first few cash reserves on upgrading your primary axe. A stronger active axe allows you to clear trees faster, accelerating your cash generation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maximize Current Plot Density">
    Purchase additional axes and place them on your starting plot. Keep adding axes until all available high-efficiency farming slots on your initial land are occupied.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Purchase Land Expansion">
    Once your starting plot is completely full and you cannot place more axes efficiently, spend your accumulated cash to purchase your first plot expansion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Deploy Advanced Layouts">
    Utilize the newly opened land to set up dedicated chopping zones for tougher targets, placing high-tier axes near stumps and giant trees.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Progression Tip">
By upgrading your axes before expanding your plot, you ensure that your newly placed axes on the expanded land are highly productive from the moment they are deployed.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Maximizing Offline Earnings with Plot Expansion">

One of the most powerful features of **my wood farm** is that your placed axes continue to farm wood while you are offline. Plot expansion directly multiplies your passive income potential by increasing the total number of axes working for you during your absence.

| Offline Step | Action | Purpose |
|--- |--- |--- |
| **Before Logout** | Upgrade axes and fill all plot slots | Maximize passive production rate |
| **Upon Return** | Claim offline wood and sell immediately | Convert accumulated resources to cash |
| **Post-Sale** | Reinvest in axes or land expansion | Keep the growth loop compounding |

<Callout type="info" title="Offline Earnings Formula">
Your offline wood gained is calculated as the difference between your wood total upon returning and your wood total before leaving. To estimate the cash value, multiply the offline wood gained by your current selling return per unit of wood.
</Callout>

</Section>
